Get to know about our company

Billy Adebudo & Co (Chartered Accountants/Tax Practitioners) was established in Nigerian and duly registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission and we are based in Lagos with a branch in Abuja.

We are a firm of Professionals providing services in Accounting, Audit and Assurance, Taxation, Financial Advisory, Corporate Turnaround and Corporate Restructuring together with corporate secretariat services including company registration and other Corporate Affairs Services.

Our team members

Billy Adebudo

Principal Consultant/Partner- HND, MBA, FCA, FCTI

Billy Adebudo is a seasoned professional, a Fellow of ICAN, and an associate member of CITN. He holds an HND in Accountancy & Finance and an MBA in General Management from Yaba College of Technology and the University of Lagos. With over 15 years of experience in financial reporting, treasury management, and accountancy, he excels in financial procedures and detailed bookkeeping, ensuring transparency and client satisfaction.

Tunde Suleiman Salam

Partner - HND, FCA, ACTI
